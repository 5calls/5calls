We're here to help. Here are a few answers to common issues.

Where can I find my congressperson’s local office number?

![](/images/troubleshooting/local-web.png) **Web** When you’re on an issue page, click "Or call a local office" under your congressperson’s big red DC phone number.
![](/images/troubleshooting/local-ios.png) **iOS** Tap “Local Offices” under the congressperson’s photo.
![](/images/troubleshooting/local-android.png) **Android** Tap “Call a local office”.

I only see one congressperson’s number. How do I call the others?

![](/images/troubleshooting/result-web.png) **Web** After you call your first congressperson, mark the result of the call. The page will reload, and you’ll see the next number.
![](/images/troubleshooting/result-ios.png) **iOS** After you call your first congressperson, mark the result of the call and you’ll see the next number.
![](/images/troubleshooting/result-android.png) **Android** After you call your first congressperson, mark the result of the call and you’ll see the next number.
## How do I change my location?
![](/images/troubleshooting/location-web.png) **Web** Click the current city or ZIP code under the 5 Calls logo.
![](/images/troubleshooting/location-ios.png) **iOS** Tap the current city or ZIP code under the 5 Calls logo.
![](/images/troubleshooting/location-android.png) **Android** On the launch screen, tap the location icon in the top right corner.

We use your location to match you with the members of Congress you need to call, so make sure to keep that info up to date.
